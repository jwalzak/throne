Nuclear Throne so many weapons. The weapons are mostly different variants of a type of weapon. Some weapons will have different effects.

Explosive Weapons

Explosive weapons are dangerous to everyone including the player. Explosions can damage the player for two to four damage on each hit. Some types of explosive weapons are:

  • Grenade Launcher
  • Bazooka
  • Flame Thrower

Although the Flame Thrower uses explosive ammo the flames do not hurt the player.

Energy Weapons

Energy weapons are weapons that use energy to fire their projectiles. The projectile can be laserss, plasma or even lightning. Some energy weapons are:

  • Lightning Cannon
  • Laser Minigun
  • Super Plasma Cannon

Shell Weapons

Shell weapons are guns that use shotgun shells for their projectiles. They fire a burst of projectiles over a wide area. Examples include:

  • Super Slugger
  • Super Flak Cannon
  • Eraser

Bolt Weapons

Bolt weapons are my favourite weapons in game. It mostly just crossbows. They do massive amounts of damage, but they usually have a slow rate of fire. Some examples include:

  • Auto Crossbow
  • Toxic Launcher
  • Super Crossbow

Melee Weapons

Walk tall and carry a big stick. In this case carry a big energy sword. Melees weapons are quite powerful. The downside is, you have to get close to the bad guys to hit them. If you hit bullets with a melee weapon it will hit it back towards the sender. This works well with the mutation Long Arms. Some examples include:

  • Shovel
  • Energy Hammer
  • Sledge Hammer
